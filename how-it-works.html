A calmer, more convenient process

1 - Find out if you quality

Learn if you are pre-approved for a fully underwritten home loan including down payment advance and start making competitive offers right away.

2 - Win your new home with a non-contingent offer

Because you’re fully underwritten by Vidi, you can make a strong, non-contingent offer to buy your new home before you sell your old house.

When your house sells, you’ll pay Vidi back for any costs covered during your move. It’s that simple.

A single, flat fee

We charge a single 6% fee, regardless of the time it takes to sell your old house. We're fully aligned with you — the higher your sale price, the more we make on commission.

Additionally, if your old house sells for less than the advance, we will take the loss. That's a promise.
